About Us

Connecting Africa through exceptional events

Our Story

Events Resources Africa was founded with a vision to transform the event landscape across the continent. We recognized the need for a comprehensive platform that connects event organizers with premium venues while maintaining the highest standards of excellence.

Over the past fifteen years, we have grown from a small local operation to becoming one of Africa's most trusted event resource companies, serving clients from corporations to cultural institutions.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing exceptional venue solutions that elevate every event experience. Our mission extends beyond simply offering spaces – we create environments where meaningful connections are forged and lasting memories are made.

Through our carefully curated portfolio of venues, we support the growth of Africa's vibrant event industry while preserving the rich cultural heritage that makes each gathering unique.
